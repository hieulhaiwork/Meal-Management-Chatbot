**Actionable Insights:**

* **Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus:**
    * Monitor blood glucose levels four times daily (before meals and at bedtime).
    * Adjust diet to include more fiber and reduce carbohydrate intake.
    * Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.
    * Consider referral to a dietitian for personalized dietary advice.

* **Hypertension:**
    * Maintain blood pressure below 140/90 mmHg.
    * Take medications as prescribed, including lisinopril, to control blood pressure.

* **Hyperlipidemia:**
    * Monitor cholesterol levels and maintain total cholesterol below 200 mg/dL, LDL below 100 mg/dL, HDL above 40 mg/dL, and triglycerides below 150 mg/dL.
    * Take medications as prescribed, including atorvastatin, to manage cholesterol levels.
